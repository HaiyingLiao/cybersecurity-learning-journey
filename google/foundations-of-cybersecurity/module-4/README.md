# Playbooks & Forensic Concepts (Introductory Level)

### Playbooks

Documents that outline step-by-step procedures for tasks such as incident response or evidence handling.

### Chain of Custody

A record of:

- Who handled evidence
- When, where, and why

Ensures evidence stays intact and trustworthy.

### Order of Volatility

A principle for preserving the most fragile data first in investigations.
