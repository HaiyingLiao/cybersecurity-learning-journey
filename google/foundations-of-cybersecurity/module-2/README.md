# Notable Historical Attacks

## Brain Virus (1986)

- Created to prevent pirated software use, but spread unintentionally.
- Infected computers via floppy disks and travelled globally in months.

## Morris Worm (1988)

- Designed to measure internet size; instead crashed thousands of systems.
- Spread by repeatedly installing itself due to poor logic.
- Affected ~10% of the internet â€” millions in damages.
- Lesson: led to the creation of **CERTs/CSIRTs** (Computer Emergency Response Teams).

### What is Social Engineering?

A manipulation technique that exploits human error to gain access, information, or money.

Attackers use:

- Authority
- Intimidation
- Familiarity
- Trust
- Scarcity
- Urgency
